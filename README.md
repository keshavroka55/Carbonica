## Project overview
Carbonica is a mobile and web platform that helps reduce carbon footprints while integrating practical environmental activities into school curricula. The platform combines a simple carbon footprint calculator with task-based student engagement, teacher verification, parental involvement, and public recognition.

## Problem
Students learn environmental theory but lack practical, graded activities that encourage real-world action to reduce emissions. Schools often lack accessible tools to track, verify, and reward student environmental efforts.

## Solution approach
The project is divided into two complementary parts:

1. Carbon footprint calculator — a simple tool that estimates daily or periodic carbon emissions from energy use, transportation, and waste.
2. School engagement platform — a system that assigns eco-friendly tasks (for example, tree planting), allows students to submit proof (photo/video), enables teacher verification, awards practical marks and points, and shows leaderboards to encourage healthy competition.

Students earn points and practical marks for verified activities. Parents can opt in to help and monitor progress. Verified achievements can be showcased on a public school portal to recognize students and promote the school's environmental efforts.

## Main features
- Simple carbon footprint calculator (energy, transport, waste)
- Real-time emission estimation
- Visual dashboards (charts, trends, insights)
- Task and challenge system for eco-friendly activities
- Practical-marks integration for schools and teachers
- Proof submission (image/video upload)
- Teacher verification workflow
- Gamification (points, badges, eco-scores, leaderboards)
- Student-vs-student and class-vs-class competitions
- Historical leaderboards for motivation
- Personalized suggestions to reduce emissions
- Scheduled reports (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Parent visibility and involvement
- Admin dashboard for school management
- Public portal to showcase student activities and impact
- Mobile and web applications
- Bilingual support (English & Nepali)
